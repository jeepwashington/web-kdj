Chicken & Beet Salad

DIRECTION

STEP 1: Whisk oil, juice concentrate, vinegar, salt, pepper and lime zest together in a large bowl.


STEP 2: Add salad greens, chicken, beets and Brussels sprouts; toss to coat. Divide the salad among 4 plates; top with goat cheese and walnuts.

INGREDIENTS

  • ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons tart cherry juice concentrate (see Tip)
  • 1 tablespoon white balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon grated lime zest
  • 1 (5 ounce) package spring mix salad greens
  • 2 ½ cups chopped or shredded cooked chicken breast
  • 1 (6.7- to 8.8-ounce) package cooked beets,
  • ¾ cup shaved Brussels sprouts
  • ½ cup crumbled goat cheese

STEP 4: Add salad greens, chicken, beets and Brussels sprouts; toss to coat. Divide the salad among 4 plates; top with goat cheese and walnuts.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 teaspoons coconut oil
  • 1 small yellow onion, finely chopped

  • ¼ teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 1 (2 inch) piece fresh ginger, peeled and finely chopped

Arugula & Cucumber Salad with Tuna

DIRECTION

STEP 1: Whisk basil, vinegar, lemon juice and salt together in a large bowl. Add oil; whisk to combine.


STEP 2: Add celery, cucumber, arugula and olives; toss to coat.

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • 1 tablespoon white-wine vinegar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons lemon juice
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • ¾ cup thinly sliced celery
  • ¾ cup thinly sliced Persian or English cucumber
  • 1 (5 ounce) package baby arugula
  • 8 pitted Castelvetrano olives

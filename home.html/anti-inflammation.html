ANTI-INFLAMMATION

Anti-inflammation strategies focus on mitigating the body's inflammatory response, which can manifest in various health conditions. This may involve incorporating anti-inflammatory foods, like fruits and vegetables rich in antioxidants, engaging in regular exercise to lower inflammation markers, and possibly utilizing medication under medical supervision to manage chronic inflammation effectively

Lifestyle changes such as regular physical activity and stress management techniques can help modulate inflammation levels. Additionally, incorporating anti-inflammatory foods like fatty fish, leafy greens, and nuts into one's diet can provide essential nutrients and compounds that support the body's natural anti-inflammatory pathways.
